About

Apart from being an MSBA student at UC Davis, John finished his bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from the Philippines in 2018. He has worked as a Global Consumer Summer Analyst (intern) for Citi Philippines under the Data & Analytics department. During his time at Citi, he developed machine learning models that predicted the propensity of customers to install Citi's mobile app – an initiative that generates savings for the company and offloads customers from analog channels. Currently, he is a practicum project data analyst for Cirrus Owners and Pilots Association (COPA) working on optimizing their search engine through the use of Python.
